TrainQuest

Interaction Design · Discovering Requirements · CPT208 Group C3-2

Project Goal

TrainQuest is a web-based gamified training platform designed to help university students build sustainable exercise habits. It supports motivation through daily challenges, rewards, progress tracking, and playful feedback. Our goal is to make training more engaging, accessible, and consistent for users who often struggle to stay active.

User Personas

Zhang Wei

20 · University Student

Background

He is a busy student who wants to become healthier but often struggles to maintain a workout routine during the semester.

Goals

Wants simple daily workout guidance, visible progress, and encouragement to keep exercising consistently.

Pain Points

Does not know what to do each day, loses motivation quickly, and feels that exercise plans are often too demanding.

Behaviour

Goals

Wants clear task instructions, motivating feedback, and a sense of achievement after each workout session.

Pain Points

Behaviour

User Journey

Stage User Action Thought / Feeling Pain Point Design Opportunity
Discovery Wants to start exercising and looks for a simple solution. Hopeful but unsure. Doesn’t know where to begin. Provide a clear onboarding and daily starting point.
Trying Searches for workout routines and compares options. Curious but confused. Too many choices and no structure. Offer guided daily challenges with simple instructions.
Using Completes a few training tasks. Motivated at first. Lacks immediate feedback. Add XP, badges, and completion feedback.
Struggling Starts skipping sessions. Frustrated and unmotivated. Routine becomes boring and easy to abandon. Use streaks, playful prompts, and visible progress.
Retention Decides whether to continue the habit. Proud if progress is visible. Hard to see long-term improvement. Show weekly summaries and growth milestones.

Problem & Solution

Problem

Many university students want to exercise regularly, but they struggle with low motivation, unclear workout planning, and a lack of immediate feedback. As a result, exercise often feels repetitive, confusing, and difficult to maintain.

Solution

TrainQuest transforms daily exercise into a playful and rewarding experience through daily missions, badges, level systems, streaks, and clear progress tracking.
